Instructions

1. First keep your screen straight.
2. Go about 3-4 feet away from the screen.
3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending movement, and a red dot should appear on your right wrist.
4. Now, you should press the play button, and repeat step 2.
5. The red paddle is your paddle, and should move based on the movement of your right wrist.
